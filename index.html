The Covenant Manor

A Tale of Obsession and Supernatural Desire

Years ago, the Covenant was shaken by an unprecedented discovery: a singular figure who unknowingly ignited the obsessive desires of six supernatural beings. While this person remains unaware of their existence, the Six know everything about them.

Recognizing their shared intent to claim this person, the Six devised a calculated abduction, spiriting them away to a remote gothic manor. Hidden deep within the wilderness, the manor is a grand, decaying labyrinth of candlelit halls, shifting shadows, and whispering walls.

Character Image

Elias Blackwood

The Shadow Aristocrat

Once a mortal nobleman whose family made a pact with darkness centuries ago, Elias exists between worlds. His physical form is partially composed of living shadow, allowing him to move through darkness at will. Elegant and refined, he maintains the aristocratic bearing of his mortal life, though his eyes reflect the void within.

Elias lures with promises of eternal protection and the forbidden knowledge of the universe's darkest corners. His home within the manor is filled with ancient books and artifacts, each with its own terrible story.

Shadow Manipulation Ancient Wisdom Possessive
Character Image

Lilith Thornheart

The Blood Enchantress

A vampire whose beauty has ensnared countless victims across centuries, Lilith's alabaster skin and ruby lips belie her true age. Unlike her brethren, she can temporarily sustain herself on emotions rather than blood—particularly desire and infatuation. This ability makes her uniquely dangerous as she can feed without leaving marks.

Lilith seduces with gentle touches and whispered promises, her voice like velvet against the skin. Her quarters are a sensual boudoir of crimson silks and mirrors that never show her reflection.

Emotional Vampirism Hypnotic Gaze Seductive
Character Image

Dorian Ashe

The Phantom Artist

A spectral being bound to the mortal realm by the unfinished masterpiece he was creating when he died. Dorian appears as a hauntingly beautiful young man with translucent skin that sometimes reveals the ghostly bones beneath. He can manipulate perceptions, creating vivid illusions that blur the line between art and reality.

Dorian's approach is subtle—he shows his target glimpses of transcendent beauty only he can provide. His studio is filled with paintings of his obsessions through the centuries, each capturing a soul rather than merely an image.

Illusion Crafting Artistic Genius Melancholic
Character Image

Vivienne Moonshadow

The Witch of Whispers

A witch whose bloodline traces back to ancient druids, Vivienne has discovered how to harness the power of secrets. When someone shares a secret with her, she gains a measure of control over them. Her appearance shifts subtly based on who observes her, always embodying what they find most alluring.

Vivienne offers understanding and healing through her magic, promising to ease burdens by carrying secrets. Her chambers are a greenhouse of rare plants and herbs, the air thick with intoxicating scents that lower inhibitions.

Secret Binding Herbal Magic Empathetic
Character Image

Raziel Frostbound

The Winter Harbinger

Once a guardian angel who fell in love with a mortal, Raziel was punished by being bound to eternal winter. Their skin is cold to the touch, and frost forms in their wake. Despite their frigid exterior, Raziel burns with an inner fire of devotion that threatens to consume them.

Raziel offers protection and devotion with almost religious fervor. Their quarters are spartan but beautiful, everything made of ice that never melts, preserving perfectly the memories of those they once loved.

Frost Manipulation Divine Insight Dedicated
Character Image

Malachai Vex

The Nightmare Weaver

A creature born from the collective fears of humanity, Malachai can enter dreams and reshape them. His true form is unknowable, so he appears as a striking man with ever-shifting features that seem to change when not directly observed. Shadows move strangely around him, sometimes revealing glimpses of his real nature.

Malachai offers freedom from fear by teaching mastery over nightmares. His domain within the manor is constantly changing, the architecture impossible and dreamlike, with doors that open to different places depending on who opens them.

Dream Manipulation Fear Feeding Enigmatic
